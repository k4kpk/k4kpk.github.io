#### Trail Guide

* **Duration**: 0.8 miles; 0:50 up; 0:30 down
* **Navigation**: 
    * Follow faint trail and bushwhack up the ridge line.  
    * Bear L at top of ridge, cross first knob to summit at 2nd knob.
